A dedicated and capable sociologist with special expertise in social network analysis and social theory. Having experience in both qualitative research and advanced statistical modelling of social networks displays my ability and interest in multi-method interdisciplinary research. My main research interests lie at the intersection of new informational/communication technologies and (ethnicized) armed conflicts. As a confident presenter and teacher of sociological subjects, I am also dedicated to continuous learning of new methods, theories and techniques.
